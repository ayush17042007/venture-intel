# Venture Intel

### AI-Powered Startup Intelligence Platform

Venture Intel is an agentic AI platform that transforms startup ideas into investor-grade business intelligence reports using real-time web research, competitor discovery, SWOT analysis, and strategic evaluation.

Instead of relying solely on LLM reasoning, Venture Intel combines live market data, specialized AI agents, and structured business analysis to provide actionable startup insights.

---

## Key Features

### Real-Time Market Research

* Live internet-backed research
* Market size estimation
* Industry trend analysis
* Target audience identification
* Opportunity discovery

### Competitor Intelligence

* Automatic competitor discovery
* Similar startup identification
* Company website extraction
* Funding information analysis
* Competitive landscape mapping

### SWOT Analysis

* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Opportunities
* Risks & Threats

### Investor Report Generation

* Executive Summary
* Market Opportunity Assessment
* Strategic Insights
* Investment Recommendation
* Key Takeaways

### Source Transparency

* Clickable references
* Research traceability
* Evidence-backed insights
* Source aggregation

### Shareable Reports

Generate public report links similar to modern AI platforms.

### Professional PDF Export

Export complete analyses as professionally formatted reports.
